# Shapelet transformation for time series classification 

A review of the paper : Hills, J., Lines, J., Baranauskas, E., Mapp, J., & Bagnall, A. (2014). Classification of time series by shapelet transformation. Data Mining and
Knowledge Discovery, 28(4), 851-881

We propose the implementation of their algorithm, relevant experimentations to highlight the advantages and also the drawbacks of their methods, and to take a step back by considering more recent work.
